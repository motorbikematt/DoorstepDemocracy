The Precincts.info Operating System

Precincts.info is a comprehensive SaaS platform built specifically for local county parties and their precinct captains.

Securing Long-Term Continuity

Our architecture ensures that institutional memory and critical voter relationships are protected. While volunteers may transition or rotate, the localized knowledge they gather remains secure with the seat, preserving continuity without restricting mobility.

Live Today & Near-Term

  • Data Fusion Layer: Open-data cohort targeting based on 26 years of public Ohio voting history.
  • AI Captain Briefings: Localized, plain-English precinct reports.
  • Geospatial Routing: Mobile-first walk maps that turn timid lit-droppers into confident door-knockers.
  • Quorum Governance Check-in (Near-Term Release): Designed to reduce administrative friction and central committee chaos, providing robust attendance verification for party meetings.
