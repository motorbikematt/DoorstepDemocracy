A New Model for Civic Infrastructure

As an Ohio Benefit Corporation, the Doorstep Democracy Benefit Company is structured to align sustainable business growth with a profound civic mission: strengthening grassroots democratic infrastructure. We believe that robust, well-equipped local parties are the bedrock of civic health.

The Two-Sided Market

Our model fundamentally shifts how campaign tech is utilized and funded:

  1. The Party (Supply & IP Owner): The county party owns its captain corps and the underlying field intellectual property generated by their hard work.
  2. The Platform (Operator): We provide the world-class SaaS infrastructure—Precincts.info—to make those captains remarkably effective.
  3. Endorsed Campaigns (Premium Tier): Rather than buying disjointed external lists, endorsed candidate and issue campaigns pay to activate this network, renting the pooled captains and field IP for targeted GOTV efforts, fully mediated by the party.

Durable asset = seat-anchored, performance-attributed field data.
