Eliminating Administrative Friction

Central committee meetings often face massive administrative overhead. We engineered the Quorum feature to mitigate this friction. By building within the explicit architectural guardrails of ORC 3599.35 (which prohibits proxy voting) and ORC 3517.03 (committee structure) [OPERATOR-REVIEW], Precincts.info provides an immutable attendance trail that smoothly aligns with statutory constraints, ending meeting-day chaos.

Strict PII Posture

We believe voter data belongs in local control. Our public dashboard is structurally inert—it displays aggregated, synthetic insights without ever exposing Personal Identifiable Information (PII). Real voter data never leaves the secure, localized architecture, distinguishing us fundamentally from legacy systems that expose sensitive lists to cloud vulnerabilities.

Security Infrastructure

Our engineering practices are built around data integrity and security from day one. As an engineering infrastructure destination target, we are designing our systems with SOC 2 readiness in mind, ensuring a rigorous roadmap toward enterprise-grade security for every local party.
