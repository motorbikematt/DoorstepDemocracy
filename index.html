The Core Problem

The grassroots organizing bottleneck is the precinct captain—an elected community volunteer operating blind, isolated, and without modern support. They face high friction tools, a blank page problem without localized context, and outdated, expensive software like NGP VAN that isolates canvassers rather than uniting them.

Our Approach

We build tools that empower organizers through deterministic cohort segmentation, localized AI-driven briefings, and mobile-first geospatial routing. We shift the initial unit of work from persuading hostile voters to recruiting safe allies and building local networks.
